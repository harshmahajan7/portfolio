About Us

Welcome to Channa Delight! We are a passionate team dedicated to bringing you the finest quality roasted channa. Our mission is to provide healthy, delicious, and flavorful snacks that cater to every taste bud.

At Channa Delight, we offer an array of roasted channa in four exciting varieties:

  • Salted: A classic and timeless flavor that everyone loves.
  • Peri Peri: For those who enjoy a spicy kick.
  • Hing: A unique and aromatic flavor that stands out.
  • Plain: Pure and simple, for the purists.

Our Commitment

We believe in maintaining the highest standards of quality and hygiene. Each batch of our roasted channa is carefully prepared to ensure it meets our rigorous standards. We source the best ingredients to bring you a snack that is not only tasty but also healthy.
